Sugar Cookies

Ingredients
3 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 cup butter
2 cups white sugar
1 egg

Directions
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. 
Mix together the butter, sugar, and egg.
Gradually blend in flour and baking powder. 
Roll into balls and place onto ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until golden.
