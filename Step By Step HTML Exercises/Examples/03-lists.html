Themes (Definition List)

Growing Up
In the eyes of a child we’ve created silly and arbitrary rules and social etiquette.
Identity
Alice struggles with the importance and instability of her personal identity.
Curiosity
Alice sees a white rabbit and decides to follow him simply because he has a watch and is wearing a waistcoat.
